const MaterialBanner({Key key, @required Widget content, TextStyle contentTextStyle, @required List<Widget> actions, Widget leading, Color backgroundColor, EdgeInsetsGeometry padding, EdgeInsetsGeometry leadingPadding, bool forceActionsBelow: false })

Creates a MaterialBanner.

The actions, content, and forceActionsBelow must be non-null. The actions.length must be greater than 0.
