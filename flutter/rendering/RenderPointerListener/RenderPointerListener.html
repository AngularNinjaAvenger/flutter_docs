RenderPointerListener constructor

RenderPointerListener({PointerDownEventListener onPointerDown, PointerMoveEventListener onPointerMove, PointerUpEventListener onPointerUp, PointerCancelEventListener onPointerCancel, PointerSignalEventListener onPointerSignal, HitTestBehavior behavior: HitTestBehavior.deferToChild, RenderBox child })

Creates a render object that forwards pointer events to callbacks.

The behavior argument defaults to HitTestBehavior.deferToChild.
